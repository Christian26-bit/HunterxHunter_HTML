Hunter × Hunter (ハンター×ハンター, Hantā × Hantā), pronounced "Hunter Hunter", is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi with two anime adaptations. The first chapter was published in March 1998, in Weekly Shōnen Jump.

The story of Hunter × Hunter focuses on a young boy named Gon Freecss, who discovers that his father, Ging Freecss, is alive and a legendary Hunter. Despite being abandoned, Gon sets out to follow in his father’s footsteps, pass the Hunter Exam, and eventually find Ging to prove himself as a Hunter.
