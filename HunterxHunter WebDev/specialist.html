Specialist (特とく質しつ, Tokushitsu).[5] Specialization (特とく質しつ系けい, Tokushitsu-kei; abbrev. as 特) can be broadly defined as any abilities that do not belong to any of the other five categories, making it the vaguest Nen type. It also appears to be the rarest.

Kurapika

A specialist with the Scarlet Eyes of the Kurta Clan. His Chain Jail is an ability that can only be used on Phantom Troupe members, showing the unique and restrictive nature of Specialization.

Neon Nostrade

Neon possesses the ability “Lovely Ghostwriter,” allowing her to predict the future through her prophecies. This is a classic example of a Specialist ability that defies other Nen categories.
