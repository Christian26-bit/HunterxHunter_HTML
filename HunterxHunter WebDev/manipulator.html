Manipulation (操そう作さ系けい, Sōsa-kei; abbrev. as 操) allow the user to control living or non-living things, including aura constructs. The main advantage of this category in combat is the ability to manipulate the enemy themselves.

Illumi Zoldyck

A master manipulator, Illumi uses his Nen to control others using special needles. His cold personality and emotionless nature make him a terrifying assassin.

Shalnark

A member of the Phantom Troupe, Shalnark uses his cellphone to control people remotely. His manipulation abilities make him one of the most versatile users in the group.
