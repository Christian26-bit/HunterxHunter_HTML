Enhancer (強きょう化か, Kyōka). Enhancement (強きょう化か系けい, Kyōka-kei; abbrev. as 強) is the ability to use aura to strengthen or increase the natural abilities of an object or one's own body. Enhancers are able to increase their physical attack and defense greatly and are best suited for melee combat. In fact, one's affinity for this category plays a crucial role in combat at all times, even when a Nen ability is not being used.

Gon Freecss

Nen Type: Enhancer

Gon is an optimistic boy with incredible potential. His Enhancer abilities give him immense physical power and straightforward combat strength.

Uvogin

Nen Type: Enhancer

A member of the Phantom Troupe known for brute strength. His Enhancer nature amplifies his raw power, making him one of the strongest physically.

Biscuit Krueger

Nen Type: Enhancer

A professional Hunter who hides her true muscular form behind a cute appearance. Her Nen enhances her physical strength immensely.

Knuckle Bine

Nen Type: Enhancer

Kind-hearted yet strong, Knuckle uses his Enhancer abilities for close combat and has a unique Nen ability called APR (Amortizing Power Redirector).
